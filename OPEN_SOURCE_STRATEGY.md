### Key Learnings
1. **Developer Experience Wins**: Best DX attracts users
2. **Community First**: Engage early, engage often
3. **Content Marketing**: Consistent, high-quality content
4. **Niche Domination**: Own your specific problem space
5. **Quick Iteration**: Ship fast, gather feedback, improve
